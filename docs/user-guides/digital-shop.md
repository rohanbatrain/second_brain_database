## Getting Started

The Digital Shop provides a marketplace for digital items including themes, plugins, templates, and more.

### Prerequisites

- An active Second Brain Database account
- Sufficient SBD tokens for purchases

### Accessing the Shop

1. Log in to your Second Brain Database account
2. Navigate to the **Shop** section from the main menu
3. Browse available items or use search/filters to find what you need

## Browsing Items

The shop homepage displays all available items in a grid layout.

### Item Cards

Each item card shows:
- **Item thumbnail** - Visual preview
- **Item name** - Title of the item
- **Price** - Cost in SBD tokens
- **Rating** - Average user rating (1-5 stars)
- **Category** - Item classification

### Navigation

- **Scroll** to see more items
- **Click** on an item card to view details
- Use the **Quick View** button (eye icon) for a fast preview

## Filtering and Sorting

### Advanced Filters

Access filters from the sidebar or filter button:

#### Price Range
- Set minimum and maximum price
- Drag the slider or enter values manually
- Click **Apply** to filter results

#### Category Filter
- Select one or more categories
- Options include: Themes, Plugins, Templates, Assets, etc.
- Multiple selections use OR logic

#### Rating Filter
- Filter by minimum star rating
- Choose from 1-5 stars
- Only items meeting or exceeding the rating will show

### Sorting Options

Sort items by:
- **Price (Low to High)** - Budget-friendly items first
- **Price (High to Low)** - Premium items first
- **Popularity** - Most purchased items
- **Newest** - Recently added items
- **Rating** - Highest rated items first

### Clearing Filters

Click **Reset Filters** to return to the default view showing all items.

## Item Details and Quick View

### Quick View Modal

Click the eye icon on any item card to open Quick View:

- **Image Gallery** - Browse multiple product images
- **Full Description** - Detailed item information
- **Specifications** - Technical details
- **Reviews** - User ratings and comments
- **Add to Cart** - Quick purchase button

### Full Item Page

Click the item card or "View Details" for the complete page:

- All Quick View information
- **Related Items** - Similar products you might like
- **Extended Reviews** - Full review section with filtering
- **Purchase History** - How many times this item was purchased

## Comparing Items

Compare up to 3 items side-by-side:

1. Click the **Compare** checkbox on item cards
2. Selected items appear in the comparison bar at the bottom
3. Click **Compare Now** to open the comparison table
4. Review features, prices, and ratings side-by-side
5. Click **Add to Cart** on your preferred item

### Comparison Features

The comparison table shows:
- Item images and names
- Prices
- Ratings
- Key features
- Category
- Purchase count

## Making a Purchase

### Purchase Flow

1. **Select an item** - Click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now"
2. **Review cart** - Verify items and total cost
3. **Confirm purchase** - Click "Complete Purchase"
4. **Payment** - SBD tokens are deducted from your account
5. **Confirmation** - Receive purchase confirmation and access details

### Payment Methods

- **SBD Tokens** - Primary currency (earn through activities or purchase)
- **Family Wallet** - Use shared family funds (if applicable)

### Purchase Limits

- No limit on individual purchases
- Family wallet purchases may require approval based on settings

## Managing Your Purchases

### Viewing Purchases

Access your purchases from:
- **Profile Menu** → **My Purchases**
- **Shop** → **Purchased Items**

### Purchase Details

For each purchase, you can:
- **Download** - Get the digital item files
- **View Details** - See purchase date, price paid, version
- **Rate & Review** - Share your experience
- **Get Support** - Contact seller or support team

### Downloads

- Downloads are available immediately after purchase
- Access download links anytime from "My Purchases"
- Some items may have download limits (check item details)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Insufficient Funds" Error

**Problem**: Not enough SBD tokens to complete purchase

**Solution**:
1. Check your token balance in your profile
2. Earn tokens through platform activities
3. Request tokens from family wallet (if member)
4. Contact admin for token purchase options

#### Item Not Appearing After Purchase

**Problem**: Purchased item doesn't show in "My Purchases"

**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your email for purchase confirmation
3. Wait a few minutes for processing
4. Contact support if issue persists

#### Download Link Not Working

**Problem**: Cannot download purchased item

**Solution**:
1. Try a different browser
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact support with purchase ID

#### Filter Not Working

**Problem**: Filters don't update results

**Solution**:
1. Click "Apply Filters" button
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try resetting filters and reapplying
4. Report bug if issue continues

## Tips and Best Practices

### Finding the Right Item

- Use **specific search terms** for better results
- **Compare similar items** before purchasing
- **Read reviews** from other users
- Check **item specifications** carefully

### Managing Your Budget

- Set a **monthly token budget** for shop purchases
- Use **price filters** to stay within budget
- **Favorite items** to purchase later
- Check for **related items** that might be more affordable

### Maximizing Value

- **Read descriptions** thoroughly before buying
- **Check compatibility** with your setup
- **Review refund policy** for each item
- **Rate purchases** to help other users
